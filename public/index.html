About the Author

Lynn M. LoPucki is the Security Pacific Bank Professor of Law at the UCLA Law School. He is the creator of VisiLaw®, a patented system for marking statutes to make them easier to read. He also maintains the Bankruptcy Research Database, an online database of large corporate bankruptcies.
